FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the e-Courts sync actually work? +

Anura tracks each matter by its CNR (Case Number Record) and polls the e-Courts portal continuously via established integrations. When a next-date, order, or listing changes, your dashboard updates and the agent notifies you - no manual checking of cause lists.

What does "Agentic Mode" do that automation doesn't? +

Automation runs fixed rules. The agent works toward a goal you set in plain language - monitoring matters, drafting routine paperwork, assembling briefs, and flagging what needs you. Every filing-grade action requires your one-tap approval, with a full audit trail.

Is my client data safe and compliant in India? +

Yes. All data is AES-256 encrypted end to end and stored on Indian servers (AWS Mumbai / Azure Central India) in line with the DPDPA 2023. Access is protected by multi-factor authentication, and every action - including the agent's - is logged for audit.

Will the Digital Briefcase work without internet in court? +

Absolutely. Briefs sync to your tablet in advance and are fully readable offline - fact summary, statutes, judgments, and your cross-examination notes - built for courtrooms with poor mobile data.

Can I migrate my existing matters and documents? +

Yes. Import matters by CNR in bulk and upload existing documents - OCR makes even scanned and handwritten files searchable. Our team assists with onboarding on Chambers and Firm plans.
