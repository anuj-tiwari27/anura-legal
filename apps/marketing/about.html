Our story

Built by people who've waited in the corridors of Court 14.

Anura exists for one reason: the practice of law in India deserves better tools than a clerk refreshing the cause list and a phone full of "any update, sir?" messages.

Our mission

Give every Indian litigator - from the solo advocate in a district court to the managing partner of a chambers - an agent that never misses a date, so their hours go to argument and advice, not administration.

We believe the next leap in legal productivity isn't another dashboard to check. It's software that does the watching, drafting, and chasing on its own, and only interrupts a lawyer when judgement is genuinely required. That is the promise of Agentic Mode.

What we believe

The principles behind the product.

Privilege is sacred

Client confidences are the foundation of practice. Encryption, Indian data residency, and audit trails aren't features to us - they're table stakes.

The lawyer decides

The agent drafts, monitors, and prepares - but every filing-grade action waits for your approval. AI assists judgement; it never replaces it.

Built for India

e-Courts, CNR, vakalatnamas, GST invoicing, WhatsApp, poor-signal courtrooms. We build for how the bar actually works here - not a port of a Western tool.
