Data Residency

Last updated: 26 June 2026

Template notice (remove before going live): Confirm the exact regions, subprocessors, and any cross-border processing against your real infrastructure before publishing.

Your data stays in India. Anura stores and processes customer data exclusively within Indian data-centre regions, consistent with the expectations of the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the preferences of the Indian legal profession.

Where your data lives

  • Primary hosting: Indian cloud regions - AWS Asia Pacific (Mumbai) and/or Microsoft Azure Central India.
  • Backups: encrypted backups are retained within Indian regions only.
  • Processing: application servers and databases that handle your matters, documents, and account data run in India.

What this covers

Data residency applies to your Customer Data - case and matter details, documents, notes, client records, and account information - as well as encrypted backups of that data.

Subprocessors & their locations

We use a small number of vetted subprocessors to operate the Service. Where any subprocessor function could involve processing outside India (for example, certain global communications or analytics providers), we limit it to non-content metadata and apply contractual and technical safeguards. A current subprocessor list, including function and location, is available on request.

Cross-border transfers

We do not transfer your Customer Data outside India for primary storage. Should any limited cross-border processing ever be necessary, we will do so only as permitted by applicable law and with appropriate safeguards, and we will update this page accordingly.

Security in transit & at rest

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). For the full picture, see our Security page and DPDPA Compliance overview.
